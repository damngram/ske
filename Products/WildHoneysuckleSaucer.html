Wild Honeysuckle Saucer

A sweet trip for picky kitties

For such a sweet smelling plant, Tatarian Honeysuckle is an invasive species in the western hemisphere. We are happy to partner with a honeysuckle bounty hunter from the wilderness of suburban Detroit to assassinate these parasites so that our feline friends can get intoxicated. Cats who don't care for catnip have been known to love honeysuckle!
