Japanese Silver Vine Saucer

Felines have fine times on the Vine

Also known as matatabi or Japanese catnip, silver vine is widely enjoyed by cats across Asia, but it is rarely found elsewhere. There is even a well-known Japanese saying about the feline attraction to silver vine. Some cats who do not express interest in catnip may fall in love with silver vine, but there is only one way to find out!
