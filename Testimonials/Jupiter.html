Jupiter

Jupiter is the type of cat we love to hear about. Check out what his cat-mom Morgan had to say about our Saucers on our Etsy page.

"When I adopted my cat, Jupiter, back in May, I was disappointed that he doesn’t react to catnip, and while I was thrilled to learn that there are catnip substitutes, I couldn’t find a way to get any. So I was quite excited when I ran across this product, and didn't waste a minute to order them. I am thrilled to say Jupiter loves his new toys: he can't get enough, and they are his new favorites! So far I've given him the honeysuckle and silver vine saucers (individually), and he's reacted brilliantly to both. He runs around with them in his mouth and chases them everywhere (and gets them lost under my stove at least five times daily). In short, these are amazing, and I’ll absolutely be recommending these to my cats-owning friends!"
