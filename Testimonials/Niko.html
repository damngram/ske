Niko

Our darling Niko was our motivation for starting Space Kitty Express. He had a tough childhood, but after adopting him and cleaning him up, we were determined to bring happiness into his life. We wanted to show him the wonders of catnip, but he was just not interested. After some research, we found several alternative cat attractants and decided to experiment on our fluffy little subject.

Niko loves rolling around with his valerian root and silver vine saucers, and we love not having to clean up little bits of herb in our carpet. Click on the video icon to see Niko playing with his silver vine saucer!
