Cats at Whiskers

We donate our toys to different cat shelters to spread joy among cats in need. Whiskers Animal Benevolent League holds a special place in our heart. Dan used to volunteer there as a teenager and he can still remember the smell of a room packed wall to wall with litter boxes. We were really happy to hear that our toys were well received and held up to vigorous play.

Remember to support your local animal shelter, we know we're going to keep sending our factory reject saucers around the country. Click on the video icon to see one cat a Whiskers turning into a cyclone in the presence of our Organic Catnip Saucer, while our dear friend Ellen tells him to be careful!
